Decorate the IM Christmas Tree!
Merry Christmas!
It is your turn to decorate the IM Christmas Tree. How will it look? What will you put on it? It is up to you!
Hover the mouse at the top of the screen to bring up the options for your decorations. Click on a tab to show you a box of decorations at the bottom left of the screen.
Click on the word on the box to open it then select your decoration by clicking and dragging it to the tree and then releasing the mouse button.
If you have put a decoration in the wrong place, double click on it (it will be highlighted in white) and then click and drag to where you want it to be.
If you want to remove a decoration, double click on it (it will be highlighted in white) and then click and drag it off the tree.
Choose as many decorations as you like!
Once you are happy with your tree hover over the bottom right of the screen to bring up the following options, right to left:
    Make it darker - click and drag the slider up and down to enjoy a christmas night or day.
    Switch on the lights - click the switch to turn on the lights on your tree.
    Make it snow - click the snowflake to make it snow, or to stop it snowing.
    Reset - click the green arrow to reset your tree.
    Play - If you are happy with everything click the play button!
To see these instructions again hover at the top of the screen and select the 'instructions' tab.
Have fun!
