Decorate the IM Christmas Tree!
Merry Christmas!
It is your turn to decorate the IM Christmas Tree. How will it look? What will you put on it? It is up to you!
Once you have decorated the tree make sure you press the PLAY button in the menu
How to decorate your tree
Click on the star at the top right to show or hide the menu:
    Present box - click to show the options to decorate your tree
    Switch - click to turn on the lights on your tree.
    Snowflake - click to make it snow, or to stop it snowing.
    Slider - click and drag up and down to enjoy a christmas day or night.
    Play - If you are happy with everything click the play button and enjoy the show!
    Reset - click the green arrow to reset your tree.
    Scroll - show these instructions again.

To decorate your tree click on a tab at the top of the screen to show you a box of decorations at the bottom left of the screen.
Click on the word on the box to open it then select your decoration by clicking and dragging it to the tree and then releasing the mouse button.
If you have put a decoration in the wrong place, double click on it (it will be highlighted in white) and then click and drag to where you want it to be.
If you want to remove a decoration, double click on it (it will be highlighted in white) and then click and drag it off the tree.
Choose as many decorations as you like!
...and don't forget to press the PLAY button to enjoy the show! Have fun!
